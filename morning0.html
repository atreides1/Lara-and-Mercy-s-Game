I had a nightmare last night. It was as vivid as it was gruesome.
I wake up sweating, shaking uncontrollably. This is normal.
Every night I go to sleep with this heavy feeling on my chest, as if somehow its contents had solidified leaving only a heavy rock pressing against the rest of me.
It’s hard to breathe. I feel dread. What is there for me to fear? Everything.
Perhaps also nothing. It’s hard to know which one it will be each day.
I have been neglecting myself lately. I don’t feel focused enough to do anything about it.
I still have time before work.
